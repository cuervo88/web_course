Welcome to

Crazy Legs Dance School

We are a newly started dance school in Oslo with a main interest in the original Swing Dance styles from 1920-1950s:

Boogie-Woogie, Collegiate Shag and Authentic Jazz.

Our mission is to develop the social dance community in Norway, and offer fun and informal dance classes for all ages so that more people can learn to dance. We believe that social swing dancing is the best hobby as it combines being active, meeting new interesting people and having lots of fun - while moving & grooving to fantastic swing, jazz and rock'n'roll music!
